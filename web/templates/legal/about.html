Our Security Measures

We take security seriously. Here's how we protect your assets:

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security to your account
  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted end-to-end
  • Cold Storage: Majority of funds stored offline in secure cold wallets
  • KYC/AML Compliance: Rigorous identity verification to prevent fraud
  • 24/7 Monitoring: Real-time fraud detection and suspicious activity alerts
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments by third-party experts
  • Insurance: Digital asset insurance coverage for added protection

Regulatory Compliance

BITOKI operates in full compliance with Nigerian financial regulations:

  • Registration: Business Name registered with Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC)
  • AML/KYC: Compliant with Anti-Money Laundering and Know Your Customer regulations
  • Data Protection: Adheres to Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)
  • Tax Compliance: Registered with Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS)
  • SEC Guidelines: Operating in accordance with Nigerian SEC cryptocurrency guidelines
