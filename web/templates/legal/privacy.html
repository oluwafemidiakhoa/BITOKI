Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: BITOKI is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register and use BITOKI, we collect:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, nationality
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, physical address
  • Verification Documents: Government-issued ID (NIN, BVN, Passport, Driver's License), selfie photos
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, transaction history, trading activity
1.2 Technical Information
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device ID
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used, click patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address
  • Cookies: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies
1.3 Transaction Information
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Transaction amounts, dates, and times
  • Trading history and gift card transactions
  • Deposit and withdrawal records

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision
  • Process your cryptocurrency trades and transactions
  • Manage your account and provide customer support
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Send transaction confirmations and account notifications
2.2 Security and Compliance
  • Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent fraudulent activities, money laundering, and terrorism financing
  • KYC/AML Compliance: Comply with Nigerian and international regulatory requirements
  • Security Monitoring: Monitor for suspicious account activity and unauthorized access
  • Legal Obligations: Respond to legal requests from law enforcement or regulatory authorities
2.3 Platform Improvement
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Test and optimize platform performance
2.4 Communication
  • Send important service updates and security alerts
  • Notify you of new features and promotions (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Conduct customer satisfaction surveys

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in these limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the Platform:

  • Payment Processors: Paystack (for NGN transactions)
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosting and database providers
  • Email/SMS Services: For sending notifications
  • KYC Verification: Identity verification service providers

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law:

  • In response to court orders, subpoenas, or legal processes
  • To comply with Nigerian Central Bank (CBN) or SEC regulations
  • To law enforcement agencies investigating illegal activities
  • To protect our legal rights or the safety of our users
3.3 Business Transfers

If BITOKI is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new entity. We will notify you before your information is transferred.

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

4.1 Technical Safeguards
  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Optional 2FA for enhanced account security
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic security assessments and penetration testing
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access controls and monitoring
4.2 Organizational Safeguards
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Confidentiality agreements with staff and contractors
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular backup of critical data
Important: While we use industry-best practices, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password and 2FA codes confidential.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 5 years (AML/KYC compliance requirement)
  • KYC Documents: Retained for 5 years after account closure
  • Support Communications: Retained for 2 years
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you withdraw consent

After retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Access and Portability
  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Explanation: Understand how we use your data
6.2 Correction and Deletion
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Account Closure: Close your account and remove non-essential data
6.3 Consent and Preferences
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of marketing communications
  • Cookie Preferences: Manage cookie settings in your browser
  • Notification Settings: Control email and SMS notifications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@bitoki.ng or use the settings in your account.

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality (e.g., session management)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use the platform
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage statistics (anonymized)
7.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

BITOKI is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from someone under 18, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside Nigeria. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place through:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions for transfers to specific countries
  • Your explicit consent where required

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting a notice on the Platform
  • Sending an email to your registered email address
  • Displaying an in-app notification

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy:

  • Privacy Team: privacy@bitoki.ng
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@bitoki.ng
  • General Support: support@bitoki.ng
  • Response Time: We respond within 7 business days
Compliance: This privacy policy complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and international best practices including GDPR principles.
